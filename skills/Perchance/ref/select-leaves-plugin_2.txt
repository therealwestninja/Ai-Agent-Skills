<h1>🌿 Select Leaves Plugin 🌿</h1>

<div style="margin:0 auto;width:100%;max-width:650px;background:white;border-radius:2px;padding:1em; box-sizing:border-box;">
	<p>This is the <code>selectMany</code> version of the <code>selectLeaf</code> plugin. See <a href="https://perchance.org/select-leaf-plugin">that plugin page for more details</a>. Very briefly: just write this in your Perchance code panel:</p>
	<p><code>selectLeaves = \{import:select-leaves-plugin\}</code></p>
	<p>And then grab 10 items from your list like this:</p>
  <p><code>\[selectLeaves(myList, 10)\]</code></p>
	<p>Or grab between 5 and 10 items from your list like this:</p>
  <p><code>\[selectLeaves(myList, 5, 10)\]</code></p>
	<p><b>Notes:</b></p>
	<ul>
		<li><a href="https://perchance.org/select-leaves-plugin-example#edit">Here's a simple example</a> showing how to use it</li>
		<li>If you want to make a <code>consumableList</code> of your leaf items so you get unique selections, check out the <a href="https://perchance.org/consumable-leaf-list-plugin">consumable-leaf-list-plugin</a></li>
		<li>Check out more plugins at <a href="/plugins">perchance.org/plugins</a></li>
	</ul>
</div>
