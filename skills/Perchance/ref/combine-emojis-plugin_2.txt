<h1>Combine Emojis Plugin</h1>
<pre>
A simple plugin to layer emojis on top of one another. Just import it like this:

<b>combineEmojis = \{import:combine-emojis-plugin\}</b>

and then

<b>combineEmojis("🚫", "🛡️")</b>

will output:

[$output("🚫", "🛡️")]

If you had a list of emojis (called "emoji", say) in your list editor, you could write:

<b>combineEmojis(emoji.selectOne, emoji.selectOne)</b>

to layer two random emojis from that list.

<hr>

<u>Technical Notes:</u>

You can also pass in an array like this:

<b>combineEmojis(\["🚫", "🛡️"\])</b>
</pre>
