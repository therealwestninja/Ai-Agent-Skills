<h1>"Tap Anywhere" Plugin</h1>

<div style="margin:0 auto;width:100%;max-width:690px;background:white;border-radius:2px;padding:1em; box-sizing:border-box;">
	<p>Put this anywhere in your HTML panel (bottom-right panel):</p>
	<p><code>\{import:tap-anywhere-plugin\}</code></p>
	<br>
	<p>Now you can tap anywhere on your generator's page to trigger randomization.</p>
	<br>
	<p><b>Notes:</b></p>
	<ul>
		<li>Works for both clicks and taps (on touch-screen devices)</li>
		<li>Check out more plugins at <a href="/plugins">perchance.org/plugins</a></li>
	</ul>
</div>
